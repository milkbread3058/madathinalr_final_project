About Us

The UNC Moonlight Dance Crew is a vibrant hip-hop dance team dedicated to showcasing our passion for dance through high-energy performances and innovative choreography. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community for dancers of all backgrounds.

Performances

We perform at various events on and off campus, including:

  • Campus events and festivals
  • Local and regional competitions
  • Charity events

Check out our YouTube channel and Instagram for highlights of our performances.

Join Us

If you're passionate about dance and want to be part of our team, we encourage you to audition! Auditions typically take place at the beginning of each semester. Stay tuned to our social media for updates.
